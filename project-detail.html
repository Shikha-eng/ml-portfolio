ICU Sepsis Prediction System

End-to-end clinical ML system predicting sepsis onset from ICU vitals.

№01

01 / OVERVIEW & PROBLEM

Sepsis is a leading cause of death in ICUs. Every hour of delayed treatment increases mortality by 8%. Early prediction is vital.

Designed a real-time prediction engine using XGBoost on PhysioNet 2019 clinical vitals (heart rate, temperature, SpO2, blood pressure) to predict sepsis onset 6 hours in advance.
